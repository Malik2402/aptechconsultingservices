Who we are

We are a comprehensive solution provider, bridging the gap between immediate technical troubleshooting and long-term strategic IT planning. Our services are structured to assist clients at every level of need, from the individual user facing a device emergency; such as a blank screen, forgotten password, or data loss; to the business owner requiring system setup and scalability advice. By offering everything from remote phone support and in-person hardware upgrades to strategic consulting, We are a reliable, all-in-one partner for both personal technology repair and foundational business IT infrastructure, ensuring our clients' devices and systems operate smoothly and efficiently.

More About Us

What we do

AP TECH Consulting Services delivers a full spectrum of technical support and IT solutions tailored to both individual consumers and businesses. Our core activities are neatly categorized into four key areas. Firstly, we provide immediate "Over the Phone Help" to diagnose and resolve urgent issues like blank screens, boot loops, and password resets. Secondly, we perform physical "Upgrades & Repairs" to address hardware problems such as slow performance, battery replacement, RAM upgrades, and overheating. Thirdly, we offer strategic "Systems & Scalability" consulting to help new or expanding businesses set up and scale their operational technology infrastructure. Finally, we ensure accessibility by being a local Durban-based resource, offering convenient contact channels via email and WhatsApp for support and service inquiries. In essence, we fix, improve, build, and support the technology that our clients depend on.

Our Expertise

AP TECH Consulting Services has cultivated a distinct expertise in end-to-end technological problem-solving and strategic growth support. Our proficiency spans from critical, hands-on technical intervention to forward-looking business infrastructure planning. Our team is skilled in rapid diagnostic troubleshooting for urgent hardware and software failures; such as boot loops, data recovery, and power issues, ensuring minimal downtime for clients. Furthermore, we possess deep practical knowledge in hardware optimization, effectively resolving performance bottlenecks through upgrades like RAM installation and overheating mitigation. Beyond device-level support, our true strategic expertise lies in designing and scaling foundational IT systems for businesses, guiding them from initial setup through expansion.
